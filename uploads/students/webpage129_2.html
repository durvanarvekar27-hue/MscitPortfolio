Why Tree Plantation Matters

Trees are essential for life. They provide oxygen, improve air quality, conserve water, preserve soil, and support wildlife. Tree plantation helps reduce global warming and creates a greener environment for future generations.

Benefits of Planting Trees

Clean Air

Trees absorb carbon dioxide and release fresh oxygen.

Climate Control

Trees help reduce global warming and cool the environment.

Wildlife Support

Forests provide shelter and food for many animals and birds.

Beautify Nature

Green spaces make cities and villages healthier and more beautiful.
