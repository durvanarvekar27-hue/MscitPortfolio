About Our Temple

Shree Divine Temple is a holy spiritual destination dedicated to devotion, meditation, and community service. The temple welcomes devotees from all religions and backgrounds.


Our mission is to spread peace, positivity, and spiritual wisdom through prayer, festivals, and charitable activities.

Temple Background Information

Temple Foundation

The temple was established in 1985 by spiritual leaders and local devotees who wanted to create a peaceful center for worship.

Spiritual Importance

The temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and symbolizes purity, meditation, wisdom, and divine blessings.

Community Service

The temple organizes free food distribution, spiritual classes, and cultural events for the community throughout the year.
