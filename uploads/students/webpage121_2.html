About Vengurla

Vengurla is a beautiful coastal town in Sindhudurg district of Maharashtra. It is famous for clean beaches, coconut trees, seafood, forts, and peaceful nature. Tourists visit Vengurla to enjoy sunsets, water sports, and traditional Konkani culture.

Top Tourist Places

Vengurla Beach

Relax on the clean sandy beach and enjoy the peaceful Arabian Sea view.

Vengurla Lighthouse

A famous viewpoint offering breathtaking views of the sea and coastline.

Shri Mauli Temple

One of the spiritual attractions known for local culture and devotion.

Alphonso Mango
