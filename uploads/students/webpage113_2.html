Welcome to Maharashtra

The Land of Forts, Festivals, and Rich Culture

History of Maharashtra

Maharashtra is one of the most culturally rich states in India. It is famous for the Maratha Empire and the great king Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj. The state has many forts, caves, and historical monuments.

“Freedom is a boon, which everyone has the right to receive.” – Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj

Famous Festivals

Ganesh Chaturthi

One of the biggest festivals celebrated with devotion and joy across Maharashtra.

Gudi Padwa

The Marathi New Year festival celebrated with traditional food and decorations.

Traditional Food

Vada Pav

The most famous street food of Maharashtra loved by people of all ages.

Modak

A sweet dish specially prepared during Ganesh Chaturthi festival.

Dance and Music

Lavani

Lavani is a traditional dance form known for energetic music and expressions.

Koli Dance

A folk dance performed by the fishing community of Maharashtra.

Tourist Places

Gateway of India

A famous monument located in Mumbai and an important tourist attraction.

Ajanta Caves

Beautiful ancient caves known for paintings and rock-cut architecture.
